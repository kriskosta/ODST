The year is 2552. Humanity is at war with the alien alliance known as "The Covenant". We are losing. The Covenant have burned our worlds, killing billions in their genocidal campaign. Earth is our last bastion - a carefully guarded secret. But not anymore. A Covenant carrier has breached Earth's defenses and attacked the African mega-city, "New Mombasa". Initial reports confirm major damage and heavy civilian casualties. Now, military leaders of the "United Nations Space Command" have prepared their response... Teams of highly-trained "Orbital Drop Shock Troopers" are ready to assault the carrier from Navy ships in low orbit. This is a near-suicidal mission. But these troopers are the best of the best. And saving New Mombasa could be the most important mission of the war...

THE MISSION

◆ CLASSIFIED BRIEFING

New Mombasa. October 20, 2552.

The Covenant have invaded Earth. Their assault carrier hovers above the African megacity of New Mombasa, and the UNSC is mounting a desperate counterattack. Your mission: drop into the heart of the battle and secure the carrier before it escapes into slipspace.

But something goes wrong. The carrier jumps while you're still in the drop pod, and the shockwave scatters your squad across the burning city. You wake up six hours later, alone in the dark, surrounded by enemies.
